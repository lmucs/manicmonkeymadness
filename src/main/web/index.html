Manic Monkey Madness (M3)
Help Page



How to Play

  • Select a weapon type by pressing the 'W' key, the rock is selected by default.
  • Click and drag to see the angle at which the cannon will shoot, release to fire.
  • Destroying enemy fortress pieces and monkeys will award points.
  • Eliminate all the enemy monkeys to win!

Projectile Types

  • Rock - The rock is a heavy projectile, it moves slow but is extremely destructive.
  • Banana - The banana is a light projectile, it moves extremely fast and is useful for direct shots.
  • Triple Banana - The triple banana shoots three light projectiles, they move extremely fast and are useful for direct shots.
  • Watermelon - The watermelon is a heavy projectile, it moves slow and explodes shortly after impact raining sweet seeds about the battlefield.

Game Modes

  • Last Monkey Standing (LMS) - It's a fight to the finish. Players must fire upon one anothers fortress until one has lost all their monkeys.
  • Demolition Derby (DB) - Wreck havoc on the enemy fortress. Gain as many points as possible by destroying monkeys and fort pieces with a limited number of shots.

Tips and Tricks

  • The "W" key changes weapon types.
  • The "P" key toggles the music.
  • The "S" key toggles all sounds.
  • The "C" key toggles the console.

Select which game mode you wish to play.

Last Monkey Standing - Play until either player has lost all their monkeys.

Demolition Derby - Select how many shots (per player) before the game ends and the player with the highest score wins. If the turn limit is exceeded, both players will be given an extra shot until there is a winner.
