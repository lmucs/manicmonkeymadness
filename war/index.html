Manic Monkey Madness (M3) Help Page

How to Play

  • Select a weapon by pressing the 'W' key, the rock is selected by default.
  • Click and drag to adjust the angle at which the cannon will shoot, release to fire.
  • Destroying enemy fortress pieces and monkeys will award points.
  • Eliminate all the enemy monkeys or get a higher score to win!

Projectile Types

  • Rock - The rock is a heavy projectile, it moves slow but is extremely destructive.
  • Banana - The banana is a light projectile, it moves extremely fast and is useful for direct shots.
  • Triple Banana - The triple banana shoots three light projectiles, they move extremely fast and are useful for direct shots.
  • Watermelon - The watermelon is a heavy projectile, it moves slow and explodes shortly after impact raining sweet seeds about the battlefield.
  • Monkey - Shoot a flippin' monkey out of the cannon! What else is there to say?

Game Modes

  • Last Monkey Standing (LMS) - It's a fight to the finish. Players must fire upon each other's fortress until one has lost all their monkeys.
  • Demolition Derby (DD) - Wreck havoc on the enemy fortress. Gain as many points as possible by destroying monkeys and fort pieces with a limited number of shots.

Controls and Tips

  • "H" opens and closes this help menu.
  • "Z" and "X", as well as click and drag, adjust the angle of the active player's cannon.
  • "W" changes the cannon's weapon.
  • "P" toggles the music.
  • "S" toggles all sounds.
  • "C" toggles the debug console.
  • "D" toggles Box2D debug draw mode. (See what's going on behind the scenes)
